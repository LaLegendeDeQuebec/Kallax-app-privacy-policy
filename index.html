Privacy Policy – Kallax Optimizer

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Overview

Kallax Optimizer respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Kallax Optimizer does not collect any personally identifiable information. All data used by the application (such as profiles, games, rooms, or configurations) is stored locally on the user’s device.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The application may access public third-party APIs to retrieve publicly available information. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or stored externally, no remote data storage or user accounts are used.
